Explore Disneyland Paris

Welcome to Disneyland Paris, where enchantment meets excitement! Immerse yourself in a world of magic and fantasy. From iconic castles to thrilling rides, Disneyland Paris offers an unforgettable experience for visitors of all ages.

Things to See

  • Sleeping Beauty Castle - Witness the fairytale charm of the iconic castle.
  • Main Street, U.S.A. - Explore the nostalgic charm of this lively street.
  • Adventureland - Embark on thrilling adventures in this exotic land.
  • Space Mountain - Experience the excitement of space exploration.

Places to Go

  • Disneyland Park - The heart of the magic awaits.
  • Walt Disney Studios Park - Discover the secrets behind the scenes.
  • Disney Village - Shop, dine, and enjoy entertainment.

Your journey doesn't end at Disneyland Paris. Explore the charming streets of Paris, the City of Light, and create memories that will last a lifetime. Uncover the magic of Disneyland Paris and the beauty of Paris itself.
